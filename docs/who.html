Who are the commentators or casters and what do they do?

Commentators

The two most important qualifications for a commentator were straightforward: an understanding of the game being commented on and the capacity to do it in an engaging way. The ability to distinguish one caster from another was also greatly aided by personality. In both cases, these were the same for both conventional and esports analysis. Giving a running commentary of a game or match is the responsibility of a commentator or caster for a broadcast. They must put the viewer's attention on what is happening at any given moment.

Shoutcasting

Casting, sometimes known as shoutcasting, is the process of explaining what is happening during a match or game in such a way that viewers can follow the action and understand what is happening at any particular moment. Additionally, the broadcaster needs to be able to engage the audience and make the broadcast entertaining. They effectively serve as home viewers' entertainment, making the game entertaining to watch.
